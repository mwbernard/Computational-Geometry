p4 - Museum Guard

by Martin Bernard and Jeonguk Choi

to run from command line after making:

"./guard"

KEYPRESS commands:

"s" - resets polygon and visible polygon, basically a restart button

"e" - press when finished drawing your polygon, allows you to place your guard
    - also stops the guard from moving

"c" - calculate and show the visible portion of the polygon. you can repeatedly click and then press "c" to move your guard around.
 
“m” - set the guard in motion

"q" - quit
